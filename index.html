Happiest Birthday, Durdona 🎂

I hope you stay happy for a long, long life.

I want to see you achieve everything you dream of.

And I want that smile to stay on your face forever.

I’ve never stood for anyone’s birthday before…

But you are different. You are special to me.

You’ve grown into a woman now…

But I still see the little Durdona in you.

I’m not good at giving gifts…

But I made this for you.

I will always stand for you…

But I know I can’t stand beside you anymore.

Even after everything… I wanted to stay.

But sometimes… wanting is not enough.

This might be the first and last birthday I’m part of…

And I regret that.

You are the kindest and sweetest person I’ve ever met.

No one can replace you.

If you ever feel low… I’ll still want to see you smile.

You are never alone.

You have people who love you… including me.

If you ever need comfort, come.

If you ever need a solution, come.

Even if I’m not beside you… I’ll still be there.

Don’t ever forget me.
