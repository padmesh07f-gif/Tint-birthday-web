Happiest Birthday, Durdona 💗

I hope you stay happy for a long, long life.

I truly want to see you achieve all the success you dream of, and I want that smile to stay on your face forever.

I’ve never stood for anyone’s birthday before… but you are different. You are special to me.

You’ve grown into a woman now, but somewhere in my heart, I still see the baby Durdona in you. Just… learn to think strong, think like a woman when life gets serious.

I’m not good at giving gifts, but I made this for you. It might not be perfect. I didn’t have much, and I didn’t even have a proper place to keep things from falling low… but I promise, I would always try to lift it up.

I will stand for you at any cost. That will never change. But I know I can’t stand beside you anymore… and that’s something I have to accept.

Even after all the fights, all the moments, I still wanted to stay. I really did. But sometimes, wanting is not enough.

This might be the first and the last birthday I get to be with you… and I truly regret that.

You are the kindest and sweetest person I have ever met. No matter what happens in life, that will never change.

I hope you won’t forget me. I’ll always be someone who feels happy seeing you succeed. I’ll always be someone who would want to lift your face up when you’re down.

You will never be alone. You have so many people who love you, care about you, worry about you… and yes, I’m one of them.

My heart was never meant to hurt you. Even when things went wrong, that was never my intention.

If you ever need comfort, come. If you ever need a solution, come. If you ever feel low, come.

And even if I’m not beside you… I’ll still be there in some way.

Don’t ever forget me.
